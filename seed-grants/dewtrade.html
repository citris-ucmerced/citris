Data Enabled Trading Networks to Sustain Agrosystems

The overarching goal of this project is to increase sustainability and profitability of agricultural systems through data enabled water trading. We have integrated four stepwise solutions to address water management challenges in basins in three states: California, New Mexico, and Utah. We propose an integrated transdisciplinary research and multi-stakeholder outreach and education program to inform, enable and encourage efficient water trading.
