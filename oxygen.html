Graduating with a great idea?

Take flight with the UC Merced Oxygen Fund. The UC Merced Board of Trustees want to untether your idea so it can fly out into the world. The new Oxygen Fund is designed to bring your idea, invention, business, class project, venture, or product to the next step. Like the air we breath, oxygen is meant to give your idea some lift and life. Receive mentoring, connections and financial support to give you runway. If you don’t design the future, who will?

Who should apply

UC Merced graduating seniors or UC Merced Alumni who graduated in the previous semester with plans to explore the viability of a project with well-defined and quantifiable goals and a target audience or user base. Open to any major.

Apply today


Two-step application process:
  • Pre-Proposal:
    The short application will take 15 minutes to complete. Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis with the first deadline set for Dec. 15, 2020.
    If selected, you’ll be invited to submit a full proposal for the spring 2021 funding cycle. Pre-proposals can be submitted at any time, are reviewed on a rolling basis, and will be notified within 3 weeks of submission whether they are invited for a full proposal.
  • Full Proposal:
    Invited full proposals received by 11:59 p.m. PST on January 15, 2021 will be evaluated and considered for the Spring 2021 funding cycle.
