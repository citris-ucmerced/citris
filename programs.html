CITRIS Programs

Our diversity of programs addresses the most pressing needs for society. Technological research and advances are delivering solutions from workforce development to entrepreneurship. As an Institute of Science and Innovation at the University of California, Merced, students make up the talent pool. Their unconventional innovation yields results in creative education programs, like NexTech Robotics, and entrepreneurial solutions.

At CITRIS UCM, in addition to our research portfolio, we focus on education and outreach programs that change the face of technology, train the next generation of tech leaders, and contribute to tech solutions in the interest of society. Our foci are:

  • Ag-Food-Tech
  • Expanding Diversity and Gender Equity in Tech (EDGE)
  • CITRIS Education & Extension
  • CITRIS Entrepreneurship
  • Student-Led Projects

Ag-Food-Tech

The University of California, Merced is in the heart of the San Joaquin Valley, one of the most agriculturally productive regions in the world and a testbed for innovation. We partner with industry leaders, growers, and natural resources managers as well as the UCANR Agricultural Research and Extension Center to develop the next generation of agricultural leaders, to establish employment-education experiences and preparation, and to invent the next technological solution to agricultural, food and natural resource issues in the San Joaquin Valley and beyond.

Expanding Diversity and Gender Equity in Tech (EDGE)

The EDGE in Tech Initiative at the University of California (EDGE in Tech) is committed to addressing the challenges faced by women and under-included identities in STEM, and specifically, engineering and computer science fields by serving as a trusted center and resource that integrates research with action.

CITRIS Education & Extension

The changing technological world requires everyone to level-up. We develop programs that not only build skills, but inspire engagement with technology at every-level.

For middle school students in Merced and the surrounding areas, NexTech Robotics introduces computational thinking in a fun, hands-on way.

For adult learners, visit CITRIS Extension to build skills in Google Earth Engine and drone piloting.

CITRIS Entrepreneurship

From Engineering Capstone projects solving real-world problems to UC Merced’s Oxygen Fund, CITRIS supports the innovation of entrepreneurs who build something from nothing. If you don’t invent it, who will?

Follow the CITRIS Foundry to receive information on upcoming trainings and presentations.

Student-Led Projects

Students are the driving force for creativity and the engine behind all our outreach and education programs. Sometimes our bright students will bring novel ideas to CITRIS and lead startups.
