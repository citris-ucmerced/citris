Ag & Food Tech Week

AgFoodTech Week aims to promote the awareness of current technologies within agriculture and the food processing industry and future issues that can be addressed between the collaboration of industry, academic, and agricultural leaders.

The week will kick off on March 1st with a Frontiers in Technology Seminar by Dr. Matthew Lange from the University of California, Davis on the Internet of Food. This will be followed by HackMerced, an annual 36-hour hackathon hosted at the University of California, Merced, where developers and designers can work together to solve pressing agricultural technology issues from March 1st - 3rd. Learn about different companies within the agricultural industry and the internship and job opportunities they offer at the University of California, Merced Career Fair on March 5th. Join CITRIS as we highlight technologies in the food processing industry at the Food 4.0 Symposium on March 7th. Finish off AgFoodTech Week by learn drone regulations at the Unmanned Aircraft Systems Safety Course hosted by the University of California Extension on March 8th.
