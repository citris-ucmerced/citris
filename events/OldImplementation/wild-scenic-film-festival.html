Wild and Scenic Film Festival

Come out to experience the Wild and Scenic Film Festival this Saturday (11/18) from 6:00 to 9:00pm at the Merced Multicultural Arts Center! It's free and open to the public! This year’s films combine stellar filmmaking, beautiful cinematography and first-rate storytelling to inform, inspire and ignite solutions and possibilities to restore the earth and human communities while creating a positive future for the next generation.
