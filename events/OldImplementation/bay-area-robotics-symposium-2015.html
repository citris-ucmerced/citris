Bay Area Robotics Symposium

The 2015 Bay Area Robotics Symposium aims to bring together roboticists from the Bay Area. The program will consist of a mix of faculty, student and industry presentations. Here are the schedule and the lineup of speakers. Symposium Chairs: Pieter Abbeel (UC Berkeley) and Marco Pavone (Stanford University). Local Arrangements Chairs: Pieter Abbeel and Igor Mordatch (UC Berkeley)
