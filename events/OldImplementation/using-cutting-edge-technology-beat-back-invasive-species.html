Using Cutting-Edge Technology to Beat Back Invasive Species

UC Davis is hosting a one-day science symposium on Invasive Aquatic Vegetation in the delta. Experts will explore new developments in surveillance and monitoring of aquatic vegetation to facilitate management and control, advances in knowledge that could lead to better management and control practices, and advances in risk assessment and reduction.
