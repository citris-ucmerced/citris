Women's Entrepreneurship Conference

Are you interested in increasing women's participation in entrepreneurial activities? Come to the Woman Entrepreneur conference on September 12, 2017, presented by the UC Merced Venture Lab. Some events to look forward to include: Hearing from multiple speakers, including Carrie Norton, founder and CEO of Green Business BASE CAMP, and Elenore Estrada, founder of Three Babes Bakeshop. Seeing a panel review of current UC Merced Venture Lab woman members and their projects. Participating in an interactive breakout session with local woman entrepreneurs.
