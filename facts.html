San Joaquin Valley Food and Agriculture Cyberinformatics

Tools and Science (FACTs) BridgeProgram


Are you entering UC Merced and interested in agriculture?

UC Merced is offering a new, innovative program to give first-year and transfer students a jump start on their education and careers in food and agriculture.


As a FACTS undergraduate researcher, you will receive:

  • a paid 6-week summer internship before you enter UC Merced
  • mentoring from Ag-Food-Tech faculty, graduate students
  • peer-to-peer mentoring
  • life skills training
  • a research experience to add to your resume
  • exposure to agricultural career opportunities
  • and more!

    • In your application, note several faculty you are interested in working with from the list below
    and we will do our best to match you to the lab or focus of your choice.
