What we are about

AgFoodTech Week aims to promote the awareness of current technologies within agriculture and the food processing industry and future issues that can be addressed between the collaboration of industry, academic, and agricultural leaders.

Student Success Job Fair

Students from the Schools of Engineering, Natural Sciences and Social Sciences, Humanities and Arts will attend the Student Success Job Fair on Tuesday, March 5th, 2019 from 11:00 AM to 2:00 PM in the Joseph Edward Gallo Recreation & Wellness Center Gym. Students seeking internships and entry-level grad careers will attend to network with industry partners and campus employers.

Executive Roundtable Discussion

The purpose of this event is to solicit feedback from key regional stakeholders engaged in agricultural production and food processing to provide UC Merced administration and faculty about the future direction of their industries, research and training needs, and trends in technologies. This feedback will serve as the basis for forging a new thrust in Ag-Food-Tech at UC Merced as it expands its mission for research, education, and outreach in the San Joaquin Valley.

CITRIS Extension Drone Safety Course

This one-day course prepares participants to take the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) Unmanned Aircraft General (UAG) test to earn a Remote Pilot Certificate with a Small Unmanned Aerial Systems (sUAS) rating. The Remote Pilot Certificate with a Small Unmanned Aerial Systems (sUAS) rating is required in order to fly drones commercially. The course will have a duration of 8 hours (including breaks). The UAG test is not included in this course. Individuals will need to register separately for the test with an FAA-approved site.
