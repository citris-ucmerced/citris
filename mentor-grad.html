Become a CITRIS EDGE in STEM Mentor!

The CITRIS Expanding Diversity and Gender Equity in (STEM) Mentoring Fellowship

Become a Summer 2021 Mentoring Fellow!

  • Mentor undergraduate students who share your interests, skills and experiences,
  • Learn how to foster valuable mentoring relationships centered on shared values,
  • 20-hour commitment over summer 2021, and
  • $925 fellowship award upon completion.

    • The CITRIS URM-STEM Mentoring Fellowship is designed to give underrepresented graduate students training and experience in how to become an excellent mentor to undergraduate students who identify as under-represented in Science Technology Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) fields using online platforms.

    APPLY

    To apply, please submit by June 1st, 2021 the following:

  • CV or Curriculum Vitae, and
  • A short essay of 700 words or less:
    1. Describe your educational and career goals and what you hope to gain from this fellowship
    2. Describe a time when you had a successful mentoring relationship (as a mentor or mentee). What made it successful?
    3. What obstacles or challenges do historically underrepresented students face in STEM careers and fields?

    Apply today

    Deadline to apply:   June 1st, 2021

    Eligibility:

  • Must be currently enrolled in a STEM graduate program at UC Merced, Berkeley or Davis.
  • Students who identify as historically underrepresented in their field are especially encouraged to apply.
