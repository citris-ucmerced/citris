Annual Report

The CITRIS Impact Report is a publication made to showcase the research, outreach and overall impact of CITRIS and the Banatao Institute. CITRIS and the Banatao Institute is home to a variety of technological, agricultural and social developments. The Impact Reports showcase the intricate work done by CITRIS within a duration of time -- typically one to three years. CITRIS' work covers many important subjects within the field of STEM research, such as: climate change, robotics, diversity and gender equity in tech and youth education, to name a few. Within the Impact Report you will find easy to digest articles explaining the institute's many projects. Accompanying these articles are pictures of the many faces behind CITRIS. While CITRIS and the Banatao Institute is a research center, it aims to use science in a way to serve its local community socially and economically.
