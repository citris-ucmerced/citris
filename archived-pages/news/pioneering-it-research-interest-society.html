What is CITRIS? Let our members tell you in their own words about the philosophy behind our work.

Find out more about the projects featured in this video and more at http://citris-uc.org/story. The Center for Information Technology Research in the Interest of Society (CITRIS) launched in 2001. Our research collaborations focus on these core initiatives: Energy, Health Care, Infrastructure, and Data and Democracy (these initiatives haven since shaped themselves into the initatives we see today: Health, People and Robots, Sustainable Infrastructues, and Connected Communities).
