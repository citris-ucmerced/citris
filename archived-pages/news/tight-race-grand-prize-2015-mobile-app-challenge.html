Tight Race for the Grand Prize at the 2015 Mobile App Challenge

This year, the CITRIS orchestrated Mobile App Challenge had a particularly tight race to win the Grand Prize of $5000. The 7 finalists went through another round of judging as part of the School of Engineering's Innovate to Grow event involving pitches and poster presentations. Honorable mentions were given to teams Pill ID and Spinewatch. In the end, team Switch with an app that can wirelessly control home appliances took home the prize.

Check out our new website, mobileappchallenge.ucmerced.edu, for more information on this and all other MAC events!
