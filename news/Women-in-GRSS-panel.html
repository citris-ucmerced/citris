UC Merced Professor presents on women in STEM at the IEEE IGARSS

  • July 31th, 2019

UC Merced Professor Erin Hestir (second from left) presented on women in STEM at the IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Society (IGARSS) in Yokohama, Japan. She was joined in the panel discussion by colleagues from Ursa Space and data science companies. The mission of the forum is to “inspire, develop, empower, and advance” diverse communities in geosciences and related fields of computer science, earth science and engineering.

Visit her website for more research updates at https://eorsblog.wordpress.com/.
