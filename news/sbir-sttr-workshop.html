Interested in submitting a SBIR/STTR Phase I application? Attend the September 25th workshop!

At the end of September, we are bringing one of the country’s leading SBIR/STTR coaches to provide 5 workshops across Central California. Roger Cohen is one of a handful of consultants that help people apply for grants from each of the federal agencies that participate in the program. His one-day workshop will help you take the next step in pursuing a SBIR or STTR grant. The cost for these workshops vary by location, The UC Merced workshop (September 25) is $45.
