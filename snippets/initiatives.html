The CITRIS Sustainable Infrastructures Initiative pursues innovation to solve our most urgent climate change needs, especially those interconnected sectors critical to the Central Valley of California, such as Food-Energy-Water nexus and just transportation for a sustainable society.
he CITRIS People and Robots Initiative focuses on new theory, benchmarks, software, and approaches that address challenges the interests of society via Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Human-Centered Computing, and cyberphysical systems.
The CITRIS Ag-Food-Tech Initiative critically responds to the needs of the region: better information, improved efficiency of inputs.
The CITRIS Health Initiative improves access to cost-effective care through the development and integration of innovative technology in telehealth, multi-lingual communication, wearables, sensors, analytics and mobile devices to improve the way we work, live and play.
